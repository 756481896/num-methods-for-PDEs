Backward Euler discretization

Backward Euler \( [D_t^- u = f]^n \) leads to nonlinear algebraic equations: $$ F(u^n) = u^{n} - \Delta t\, f(u^n, t_n) - u^{n-1}=0,$$

Alternative notation: $$ F(u) = u - \Delta t\, f(u, t_n) - u^{(1)} = 0\tp$$
